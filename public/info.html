About This Site

This project is a fan-made archive that catalogs collectible crate items from the SimpleSurvival Minecraft server.

Created for passionate players, it serves as a visual reference for cosmetics, seasonal events, and special edition items over time. There may be a few missing or inaccurate items—if so, please fill out the contact form below to notify the developer.

About the Developer

I’m YooEm (also known as Josh), a software development student focused on building meaningful web projects that have a positive impact on online communities.

SimpleSurvival Collectibles is my first major web development project and is currently a work-in-progress. I'm passionate about creating engaging web experiences and excited to continue growing through future projects.
